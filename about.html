About US

The places where guys can feel comfortable and receive great services along with excellent grooming are always in demand and popular.

Keeping this in mind, we decided to create a special barber shop where all the traditions of classical barbering will be preserved.

At Billy, experience begins when you walk through the door. We have created the perfect atmosphere where you can relax and get your desired haircut.

The smell, the music, the décor – everything will take you back to a time when taking care how you looked was a part of being a man, a true gentleman.

Our main goal is to get to know our clients and what they desire. You don’t have to make any appointments, just visit us when you have time and one of our barbers will take care of you.
